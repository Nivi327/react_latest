Introduction:

For the convenience of the enthusiastic participants of Esparx 2023, we here in the hospitality department of JNTUK take it upon ourselves to provide you with a convenient stay place within the institute itself, owing to the huge scale of participation, we have established a set of procedural guidelines to help the attendees make the process seamless and without glitches.

Let's now walk you through them stepwise.

Instructions:

  • The student participants are required to carry their valid college photo id card. Any attendee failing to produce their id card will not be permitted inside the campus during the Esparx 2023.
  • Alcohol, drugs and other unauthorized materials are not allowed.
  • No parties are allowed in the premises of the campus.
  • All attendee's are requested to maintain the decorum and cleaniness of the campus, and follow the rules of the campus all the time.
  • We, organizers of the event, are not responsible for the loss of your valuable items. In case of carrying them, please take care of your valuables.
  • All the attendees who does not have accomodation inside the campus will not be allowed to stay in the campus after 10pm.
  • Only the attendees/guests having their accomodation in the campus will be allowed in the campus premises after 10pm.
  • Make sure to maintain the Esparx event Id card which is used for the verification purpose by the co-ordinators.
  • Please note that the check in and key collection takes place at Jntuk, Esparx 2023, Help desk.
  • In case of any emergency, please contact the organizers mentioned below.
